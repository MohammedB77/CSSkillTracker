AI-Powered Career Path Advisor for Computer Science Students
---

Job Skills Tracker is an AI-powered web application that helps Computer Science students and aspiring software engineers choose the right career path by providing smart recommendations based on real job market requirements, including the most in-demand skills, frameworks, tools, and certifications.

---
## Features

- Technology Career Explorer — Explore different technology career paths and understand what each role requires.
- AI-Powered Career Insights — Receive AI-powered insights based on your selected technology career path.
- In-Demand Skills — Discover the skills employers are actively seeking in today's job market.
- Technology Stack — Explore the programming languages, frameworks, libraries, and tools commonly used in your target role.
- Certification Guidance — Find industry-recognized certifications that support your professional growth.
- Market Insights — Explore up-to-date job market trends and hiring requirements for your selected career.
